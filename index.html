How this tracker works

Tap the checkbox to mark a hand complete. Completed hands stay in place and turn gray.

Tap anywhere on a hand row to mark it as your current hand. It gets a yellow highlight.

Clear Current Hand removes only the yellow current-hand highlight.

Clear Checkmarks removes only completed checkmarks.

Reset All clears both checkmarks and the current-hand highlight.
